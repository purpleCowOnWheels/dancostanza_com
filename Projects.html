  3. Why Hire Me?
    I'm a grinder. I learned how to work growing up on a farm in Pennsylvania where your job is to get it done. There are no excuses if the chickens get eaten. Sometimes this is done by coming up with the clever way, but equally often by knowing when to just get your hands dirty and do it.

    I went to Williams college where I rowed internationally against opponents who averaged 4 inches and 50 pounds larger than I, and competed by spending 6 hours a day in the gym, doing math problem sets at 135 bpm on a bike. I'm not the best mathematician, but I succeed with creativity, hard work and the ability to see comparbility in seemingly dispareate projects.

    To an office, I bring a history of top performance. I find interesting, high impact projects, get excited about them, use my math and cs to solve the parts I can, am unafraid to learn the things I don't, and know when to turn on the farmboy switch and simply grind and execute.

    In short, you can go get another Harvard kid who looks the part and might crush me on a math test, but if you want interesting and business generating ideas, executed effectively and on time, you should hire me instead.
